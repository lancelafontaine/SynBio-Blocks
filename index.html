How to play: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When two tiles with the same image touch, they merge into one! Tile by tile, atom by atom, you can use the power of synthetic biology to create a genetically-engineered microalgae capable of producing valuable products such as biofuels. Note: This game is a derivative of the official version of 2048. You can play the original game here. If you would like to find out more about our team or our project, visit our wiki.
